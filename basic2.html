The Certificate of the BASIC II Course proves the ability of interacting the right way in the following situations:

• Greet and introduce people
• Ask and offer some personal information about living, nationality, date and place of birth, marital status, occupations, family, workplace, daily routine, abilities and experiences
• Accept and refuse invitations
• Talk about future plans and activities
• Describe people, places and events
• Ask and offer help
• Compare cities
• Talk about locations and ask for directions
• Talk about personal hopes and ambitions
• Ask and give suggestions
• Make comparisons
• Discuss festivals and trips
• Discuss life past events
