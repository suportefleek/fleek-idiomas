The Certificate of the TEENS I Course proves the ability of interacting the right way in the following situations:

• Ask and give personal information about living, daily routine, birth data, marital status, occupations, family, workplace, abilities and experiences
• Invitations, accepting and refusing them
• Describe people, places and events
• Agreeing and disagreeing
• Describe parts of a house
• Ask and answer about seasons and seasonal events
• Talk about countries and nationalities
• Describe clothes
• Ask for food and drinks
• Talk about time
• Talk about common possessions
• Discuss prices and numbers
